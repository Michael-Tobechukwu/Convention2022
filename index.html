Got Questions? Find Answers Here!

How do I register for the convention?

Registration for the convention can be done online through our website. Simply click Register Now and follow the instructions to secure your spot!

What is the convention theme about?

The theme 'Back to Bethel' signifies a spiritual journey of reawakening and revival. It's a call to return to our roots and experience a deeper connection with God.

How long will the convention last?

The Convention is scheduled to run from Monday, July 15 to Friday July 19, 2024 (that's 5 days), with different events scheduled for each day, including Skills Acquisition Trainings, Powerful Word and Worship Sessions, Leadership Summit, and much more.
Click here to learn more

Can I bring my children to the convention?

Absolutely! They're not too young to be touched by the power of God and to be used for signs and wonders in their generation. Bring your children along!

Will there be food available at the convention?

Yes, we will have a variety of food and snacks throughout the convention. You won't go hungry at LP10 YAYA Convention 2024!
