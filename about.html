About RCCG LP10 YAYA

Who We Are

RCCG LP10 YAYA is the umbrella body of all youths and young adults in The Redeemed Christian Church of God Lagos Province 10, Agege, Lagos.
Our Provincial Youth Pastor is Pastor Tope Omojola, who is also the Regional Youth Pastor of Region 37 YAYA.
Our Provincial Youth President is Bro. Michael Williams.
Our provincial headquarters is at 2, Somoye street, Omotoye estate, Mulero, Agege, Lagos.

What We Do

We hold various programmes throughout the year to equip youths and young adults within the province for today's challenges and tomorrow's realities. Our programmes are aimed at all-round growth - from spiritual and mental, to physical, financial and social.
