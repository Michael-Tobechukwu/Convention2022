About RCCG LP10 YAYA

Who We Are

LP10 YAYA is the umbrella body of all youths and young adults in The Redeemed Christian Church of God Lagos Province 10, Agege, Lagos. Our headquarters is at 2, Somoye street, Omotoye estate, Mulero, Agege, Lagos.

What We Do

We hold various programmes throughout the year to equip youths and young adults within the province for today's challenges. Our programmes are aimed at all-round growth - from spiritual and mental, to physical and social.
